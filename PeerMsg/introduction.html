Introduction to PeerMsg



PeerMsg is a message board built with a peer-to-peer network architecture.



Rationale: We realised that many traditional message boards suffer from a few of the following problems as a result of their reliance on a server.

  • They are slow or inaccessible during peak hours due to the servers being unable to cope with the heavy load of having so many users log onto the servers at the same time, causing massive inconvenience when the message board is most needed.
  • They are inaccessible during server maintenance or breakdowns as the entire system cannot function without a server. All of the processing of client-server networks are done by the servers so without it the network is just a bunch of computers connected together without much purpose.
  • Large, clunky and expensive servers are required for such message boards to be set up. Due to this limitation, many small startup companies are unable to pay for or find space for the servers.


Scaleability of peer-to-peer architecture.

Our Aim: To create a peer-to-peer messaging board that is more reliable and faster than traditional message boards based on the client-server model.



Target Audience: Generally, this project is targetted at all organisations and schools which have the need for quick and efficient message dissemination across a large number of people.

More specifically, this PeerMsg can be extremely helpful to smaller companies or organisations that may not have the necessary funds or space to acquire a server to setup such a message board.
